RateFlip Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 9, 2026

RateFlip ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Currency Conversion Input: When you enter amounts for currency conversion, the values are processed locally on your device and are not stored on our servers.
  • Exchange Rate Data: We fetch real-time exchange rates from third-party APIs to provide accurate currency conversions.
  • App Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics (such as which currency pairs are most frequently used) to improve our service.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic device information (such as device type and operating system) to optimize app performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide currency conversion services
  • Improve and optimize the app experience
  • Analyze app performance and usage patterns
  • Maintain the functionality of our service

3. Data Storage and Security

Your currency conversion inputs are processed locally on your device and are not persistently stored on our servers. Any cached exchange rate data is stored temporarily to improve app performance. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information.

4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party APIs (such as exchange rate providers) to fetch real-time currency data. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. We may share anonymous, aggregated data with third parties for analytics purposes.

6. Your Choices

You can:

  • Clear the app's cache at any time through your device settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Contact us if you have questions about your data

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy
